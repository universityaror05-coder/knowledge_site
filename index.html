Grammar & Vocabulary Lessons

1. Present Simple Tense

Use the present simple for facts and habits.

Example: "I work every day."

2. Past Simple Tense

Use the past simple for completed actions.

Example: "She visited Paris last year."

3. Future with 'Will'

Use 'will' for predictions and promises.

Example: "I will call you later."
